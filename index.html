Campus Life

The Mother’s International School is sited amidst the sylvan surroundings of Sri Aurobindo Ashram (Delhi branch) campus on Sri Aurobindo Marg. It offers well-equipped laboratories in all departments for better understanding of the subjects. Apart from the hi-tech computer lab, the school offers a book store, library, auditorium and cafeteria. Sports facilities include a 400m athletics track, two basketball courts, three badminton courts, two volleyball courts, table tennis room with eight tables, a gymnasium, cricket nets, and football field. The school’s computer club, MINET (MIS Information Network) maintains the school website.
